Act 1

Scene 1

In the beginning of this scene two members of the house of Capulet, Sampson and Gregory boast about random stuff. Two servingmen of the house of Montague, Abram and Balthasar enter and Sampson and Gregory immediately draw their weapons. The Montagues and Capulets argue and Benvolio enters the scene Sampson and Gregory fight with Abram and Balthasar and Benvolio stops them. Tybalt then enters and fights Benvolio, other Capulets and Montagues join the brawl. The brawl is stopped by officers and Capulet and Maontague enter with their wives. Prince Escales then arrives and scolds everyone involved in the brawl, saying that they will be killed if they disturb the peace again. Everyone leaves except Montague, Lady Montague, and Benvolio, they discuss what happened and Lady Montague asks where Romeo is and they discuss his recent sadness, Romeo then enters the scene and Montague and Lady Montague exit. Alone, Benvolio and Romeo discuss Romeo's sadness and Benvolio learns it is because Rome is in love with Rosaline, the daughter of Capulet.

Scene 2

this scene starts with Capulet talking to Paris about him and Montague being bound by the threat given by Prince Escales. The conversation quickly changes to Paris marrying Juliet. Capulet invites Paris to a family ball and sends his servant out to deliver invitations. The servant leaves but cannot read so he sets out to find someone who can. benvolio and Romeo are arguing when the servant comes and asks if Romeo can read. There is some confusion but Romeo reads the invitation for the servant. The servant thanks him for his help and invites him to the party.

Scene 3

in this scene, Lady Capulet tells Juliet about her arranged marriage to paris. juliet agrees to give him a chance. at the end of the scene, a servingman comes in to tell Lady Capulet that the guests for the party have arrived.

Scene 4

in this scene, benvolio and Mercutio try to convince Romeo to come to a masquerade with them. Romeo refuses because he is going to Capulet's party because the servant invited him

Scene 5

this scene begins with four servingmen scrambling around making preperations for the party. soon after, all of the guests arrive. capulet welcomes the guests and the party begins. romeo asks a servant who juliet is but the servant does not know. tybalt recognises that romeo is a montague and tells his servant to fetch his rapier but capulet stops him from starting conflict, calling him a saucy boy. romeo speaks to juliet and kisses her twice immediately. romeo speaks to nurse and finds out that juliet is a capulet, then juliet speaks to nurse and finds out that romeo is a montague.
